Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Bana Private Limited · Singapore

1. Introduction

Bana Private Limited ("BANAHub", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the BANAHub platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity data: name, email, LinkedIn profile, institutional credentials
  • Business data: company profiles, financial information, raise details
  • Usage data: access logs, page views, feature usage
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Communications: enquiry messages, support correspondence

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your institutional credentials and process your application
  • To match you with relevant opportunities, companies, and investors
  • To send platform notifications and updates
  • To process payments and maintain billing records
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To detect fraud and maintain platform security

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With other members as governed by the access control system (member, request-only, admin levels)
  • With service providers strictly for platform operation (Supabase, Stripe, Resend)
  • Where required by law or regulatory authority
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement row-level security (RLS) on all private data. Sensitive fields (raise amounts, cap table, investor mandates) are encrypted and accessible only through our approval workflow. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We conduct access audits and maintain audit logs of all data access events.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services. Upon account deletion, data is removed within 90 days unless retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and correction of your personal data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data portability on request
  • Withdrawal of consent where processing is consent-based

8. Cookies

We use functional cookies for authentication (JWT tokens stored in localStorage). We do not use third-party advertising cookies.
